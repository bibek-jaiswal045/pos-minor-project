Feature Overview

Product Management

  • Easily add new products and update existing ones with comprehensive details such as name, category, price, and stock levels.
  • Organize products into categories for simplified management and quick access.

Inventory Tracking

  • Monitor stock levels in real-time to ensure you never run out of popular items.
  • Make adjustments to stock quantities to account for returns, damages, and discrepancies.

Customer Management

  • Maintain detailed customer profiles with contact information, purchase history, and preferences.
  • Implement and manage customer loyalty programs to reward repeat purchases and build customer loyalty.
